How we scored this

Each request was scored from −5 to +5 based on how the agency handled it. The transparency score is the average across all requests for that town.

  • +5Immediate electronic access, little to no redaction
  • +4Timely access with limited redaction
  • +3Delayed access after back and forth
  • +2Access, but required in-person pickup or residency
  • +1Access with excessive redaction
  • 0No responsive documents exist
  • −1Process confusion, documents not provided
  • −2Rejection on records requiring balancing test
  • −3Acknowledged but never fulfilled
  • −4Rejected records routinely considered public
  • −5No response at all
